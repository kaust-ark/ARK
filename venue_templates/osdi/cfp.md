# OSDI '25 — Call for Papers

**Source:** https://www.usenix.org/conference/osdi25/call-for-papers
**Full Name:** 19th USENIX Symposium on Operating Systems Design and Implementation
**Conference Dates:** July 7–9, 2025, Boston, MA (co-located with USENIX ATC '25)

## Key Dates

| Milestone | Date |
|---|---|
| Abstract registration | December 3, 2024, 5:59 PM EST |
| Paper submission | December 10, 2024, 5:59 PM EST |

| Final papers due | June 5, 2025 |

## Page Limit & Format

- **Submission:** Maximum 12 single-spaced pages (figures/tables included); references unlimited
- **Accepted papers:** 14 pages + unlimited references in proceedings
- Appendices/supplementary material optional; PC not required to read; paper must stand alone
- US Letter (8.5"×11"), two-column, 10pt Times Roman, 7"×9" text block, 0.33" column separation
- Page numbers required; figures must be legible in black & white
- PDF only; strict compliance required (violations → rejection without review, no reformatting extensions)

## Topics

Operating systems, File & storage systems, Distributed systems, Cloud computing, Mobile systems, Secure & reliable systems, Systems aspects of big data, Embedded systems, Virtualization, Networking (as it relates to OS), Interfaces with computer architecture, PLs, analytics, databases.

## Anonymity

Double-blind. Good-faith anonymization required. Anonymized arXiv preprints permitted. Papers with NDAs → rejected. Contact osdi25chairs@usenix.org if uncertain.

## Other Policies

- Author response: max 2,000 words; correct factual errors or answer reviewer questions only
- Simultaneous submissions prohibited
- Artifact evaluation available for accepted papers
- Submission site: https://osdi25.usenix.hotcrp.com/
