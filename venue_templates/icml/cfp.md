# ICML 2025 — Call for Papers

**Source:** https://icml.cc/Conferences/2025/CallForPapers
**Conference Dates:** July 13–19, 2025, Vancouver, Canada

## Key Dates

| Milestone | Deadline |
|---|---|
| Abstract submission | January 23, 2025 AoE |
| Full paper submission | January 30, 2025 AoE |

## Page Limit & Format

- **Main body:** 8 pages (references, impact statement, appendices excluded)
- Accepted papers: +1 page (9 pages) for camera-ready
- US Letter, two-column, Times 10pt, Type-1 fonts only, PDF
- Template: https://media.icml.cc/Conferences/ICML2025/Styles/icml2025.zip

## Topics

General ML, Deep Learning, Evaluation, Learning Theory, ML Systems, Optimization, Probabilistic Methods, Reinforcement Learning, Trustworthy ML (fairness, interpretability, privacy, robustness, safety), Application-driven ML (healthcare, physical sciences, biosciences, sustainability).

## Anonymity

Double-blind. No author names/affiliations/acknowledgements. Authors may post preprints but must not advertise as ICML submissions during review.

## Other Policies

- Mandatory **Broader Impact Statement** (unnumbered, outside page limit)
- Reciprocal review: qualified authors must agree to review
- In-person presentation required for accepted papers
- LLM use permitted; authors bear full responsibility
