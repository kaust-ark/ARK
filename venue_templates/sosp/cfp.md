## Page Limit & Format

- **Main body:** Maximum 12 pages (text, figures, tables); references excluded
- A4 or US Letter, 178×229 mm centered text block, two columns, 8 mm column spacing
- 10pt Times Roman / Linux Libertine on 12pt (single-spaced) leading
- All pages numbered; internal references hyperlinked
- Color figures encouraged (must be readable in grayscale)
- **Recommended LaTeX template:**
  ```latex
  \documentclass[sigplan,10pt]{acmart}
  \renewcommand\footnotetextcopyrightpermission[1]{}
  \pagestyle{plain}
  \settopmatter{printfolios=true}
  ```

## Topics

Operating systems, File & storage systems, Distributed systems, Cloud computing, Mobile & edge systems, Secure & reliable systems, Systems for big data & ML, Embedded & real-time systems, Virtualization, Networking (OS-related), Management & troubleshooting of complex systems, Interactions with computer architecture, PLs, analytics, verification, databases.

## Anonymity

Double-blind. Good-faith anonymization: use paper ID instead of author names, reference own prior work in third person, anonymize known project/system names. Concurrent submissions must be cited anonymously (email PC chairs).

## Other Policies

- Author response max ~500 words; factual corrections/reviewer questions only
- arXiv and technical reports explicitly permitted
- Workshop publication permitted if significant additional content exists
- Supplementary material optional; PC not required to read it
- Artifact evaluation expected for accepted papers
- Submission site: https://sosp25.hotcrp.com/
