# NeurIPS 2025 — Call for Papers

**Source:** https://neurips.cc/Conferences/2025/CallForPapers
**Conference Dates:** November 30 – December 7, 2025, San Diego, California

## Key Dates

| Event | Date (AoE) |
|---|---|
| Abstract submission | May 11, 2025 |
| Full paper submission | May 15, 2025 |
| Supplemental materials | May 22, 2025 |
| Author rebuttal | July 24–30, 2025 |
| Author notification | September 18, 2025 |
| Camera-ready | October 23, 2025 |

## Page Limit & Format

- **Main paper:** 9 pages (figures/tables included)
- **References, NeurIPS checklist, appendices:** unlimited, excluded from limit
- Accepted papers: +1 page for camera-ready
- Must use official **NeurIPS 2025 LaTeX style file**; non-compliant submissions may be desk rejected
- PDF, max 50 MB

## Topics

Applications, Deep Learning, Evaluation, General ML, Infrastructure, ML for Sciences, Neuroscience/Cognitive Science, Optimization, Probabilistic Methods, Reinforcement Learning, Social/Economic Aspects, Theory. Interdisciplinary work encouraged.

## Anonymity

Strict double-blind. No author info anywhere in paper/supplemental/links. Violations → desk rejection. arXiv preprints allowed; do not label them "Under review at NeurIPS."

## Other Policies

- OpenReview profile required (update before full paper deadline)
- Mandatory NeurIPS paper checklist on reproducibility/ethics (excluded from page limit)
- Dual submissions prohibited
- LLM usage: authors bear full responsibility
