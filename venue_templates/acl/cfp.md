# ACL 2025 — Call for Papers

**Source:** https://2025.aclweb.org/calls/main_conference_papers/
**Conference:** July 27 – August 1, 2025, Vienna, Austria
**Theme:** Generalization of NLP Models

## Key Dates

| Milestone | Date (AoE) |
|---|---|
| Paper submission to ARR | February 15, 2025 |
| Author response period | March 27–April 3, 2025 |
| Reviews & meta-reviews available | April 15, 2025 |
| ACL 2025 commitment deadline | April 20, 2025 |
| Acceptance notification | May 15, 2025 |
| Camera-ready | May 30, 2025 |

## Page Limit & Format

| Type | Submission | Camera-Ready |
|---|---|---|
| Long paper | 8 pages | 9 pages |
| Short paper | 4 pages | 5 pages |

- References, Limitations section, ethical considerations: excluded from limit
- Use official ACL style template; violations → desk rejection
- **Limitations section is mandatory** (omission → desk rejection)

## Submission Process

Two-stage via ACL Rolling Review (ARR): (1) submit to ARR Feb cycle, (2) commit to ACL 2025 by April 20.

## Topics (22 areas)

Computational Social Science, Dialogue & Interactive Systems, Discourse & Pragmatics, Efficient/Low-Resource NLP, Ethics/Bias/Fairness, Generation & Language Modeling, Human-Centered NLP, Information Extraction/Retrieval, Interpretability, ML for NLP, Machine Translation, Multilinguality, Multimodality, NLP Applications, Phonology/Morphology, Question Answering, Resources & Evaluation, Semantics, Sentiment Analysis, Speech, Summarization, Syntax. Special theme: Generalization of NLP Models.

## Anonymity

Double-blind. **No anonymity period** — preprints may be posted at any time. Remove author names/affiliations/self-identifying references from submission.

## Other Policies

- Responsible NLP Research Checklist required
- Dual submissions prohibited
- All authors must register on submission platform within 48h of deadline
