## Anonymity

Double-blind. No identifying info in paper, acknowledgments, supplementary, or external links. Violations → rejection without review. All authors must complete OpenReview profiles by submission deadline.

## Other Policies

- Author list final after submission deadline (no additions/reordering)
- Dual submission prohibited (same work at another venue during Nov 14 – Feb 26)
- Mandatory reviewer participation for authors
- LLM use permitted; authors responsible for accuracy and citations
- Media embargo: do not publicize as "submitted to CVPR" until acceptance
