# MLSys 2025 — Call for Papers

**Source:** https://mlsys.org/Conferences/2025/CallForPapers
**Conference Dates:** May 12–15, 2025, Santa Clara Convention Center, CA

## Key Dates

| Event | Date (UTC) |
|---|---|
| Submission opens | October 10, 2024 |
| Submission deadline | October 31, 2024, 20:00 UTC |
| Author response period | January 20–27, 2025 |
| Author notifications | February 10, 2025 |
| Camera-ready | April 7, 2025 |

## Page Limit & Format

- **Main body:** Up to 10 pages (references excluded)
- Appendices: unlimited, reviewers not required to read
- Two-column layout using `mlsys2025style.zip`
- Submit via OpenReview

## Topics (17 areas)

Efficient model training/inference/serving, LLM training/fine-tuning/inference, Compound AI & agent systems, Distributed & federated learning, Privacy & security for ML, ML for job scheduling, Testing/debugging/monitoring of ML, Fairness/interpretability/explainability, Data preparation & cleaning, ML programming models & abstractions, ML programming languages, ML compilers & runtimes, Visualization of data/models/predictions, Specialized hardware for ML, LLM-based hardware design/system optimization, Hardware-efficient ML methods, ML benchmarks/datasets/tooling.

## Anonymity

Double-blind. Authors may post to arXiv; must not take steps to identify themselves.

## Other Policies

- LLM-generated content in submissions prohibited (consulting LLMs is fine)
- Artifact evaluation: voluntary, follows ACM Artifact Review and Badging policy
- Dual submissions (under review elsewhere or previously published) prohibited
