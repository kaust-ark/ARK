# EMNLP 2025 — Call for Papers

**Source:** https://2025.emnlp.org/calls/main_conference_papers/
**Conference Dates:** November 5–9, 2025, Suzhou, China

## Key Dates

| Milestone | Date (AoE) |
|---|---|
| ARR submission deadline | May 19, 2025 |
| Reviewer registration | May 21, 2025 |
| Reviews due | June 18, 2025 |
| Author response/discussion | June 26 – July 2, 2025 |
| Commitment deadline | August 1, 2025 |
| Acceptance notification | August 20, 2025 |
| Camera-ready | September 19, 2025 |

## Page Limit & Format

| Type | Submission | Camera-Ready |
|---|---|---|
| Long paper | 8 pages | 9 pages |
| Short paper | 4 pages | 5 pages |

- References, appendices, supplementary: excluded from limit
- Use official ACL/ARR LaTeX or Word style files
- **Limitations section mandatory**

## Submission Process

Two-stage via ACL Rolling Review (ARR): (1) submit to ARR May 2025 cycle by May 19, (2) commit reviewed paper to EMNLP by August 1.

## Topics (30 areas)

Safety & Alignment in LLMs, AI/LLM Agents, Human-AI Interaction, RAG, Mathematical/Symbolic Reasoning, Computational Social Science, Code Models, Interpretability, LLM Efficiency, Generalizability, Dialogue Systems, Discourse/Pragmatics, Low-resource NLP, Ethics/Bias/Fairness, NLG, Information Extraction, Linguistic Theories, Machine Translation, Multilinguality, Multimodality, Neurosymbolic NLP, Phonology/Morphology, QA, Resources/Evaluation, Semantics, Sentiment Analysis, Speech Processing, Summarization, Syntax, NLP Applications. Special theme: Interdisciplinary Recontextualization of NLP.

## Anonymity

Double-blind. **No anonymity period** — arXiv preprints allowed at any time. Remove author names/affiliations from submission.

## Other Policies

- Responsible NLP Research Checklist required
- Dual submissions not allowed
- LLM writing assistance: disclosure required; authors bear full responsibility
